Who is Mendee for

Mendee is everyday Nigerians who need access to skilled people who can provide them with everyday service at their convenient time. Mendee is for skilled artisans, people who can provide errand services, skilled and trained

For Customers

  • You're guaranteed a refund if the mender doesn't provide service agreed upon
  • You get to scan through various mender options and choose based on ratings by other people
  • Full details of the Mender would be sent to your email upon successful booking.

For Artisans

  • Registration is totally free
  • You get notifications of users trying to book you and you get to accept whichever service you're comfortable offering
  • Payment made to you on completion of a successfully delivered service

For Erranders

  • Registration is totally free
  • You get notifications of users trying to book you and you get to accept whichever service you're comfortable offering
  • Payment made to you on completion of a successfully delivered service
